Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our junk removal and demolition services.

How much does junk removal cost in San Diego?

Junk removal in San Diego typically starts around $99 for small loads (1–5 items) and ranges up to ~$400 for a full pickup truck. Final pricing depends on volume, weight, and type of materials. We provide free upfront quotes with no hidden fees — you only pay when the job is done.

Do you offer same-day junk removal in San Diego?

Yes! Same-day junk removal is available in San Diego, subject to schedule availability. Text or call us at (619) 841-4193 and we'll do our best to dispatch a crew the same day. We serve all of San Diego County Monday through Saturday, 8am–7pm.

What areas of San Diego County do you serve?

We serve all of San Diego County, including San Diego, Chula Vista, El Cajon, La Mesa, Santee, Spring Valley, National City, Lemon Grove, Poway, Escondido, Oceanside, Vista, San Marcos, Lakeside, Alpine, and Coronado.

Are you licensed and insured for junk removal in San Diego?

Yes. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We hold CA Contractor License #1135398 and a D-63 Post Construction & Demolition Cleanup certification, covering all junk removal and demolition services throughout San Diego County.

What items do you NOT remove?

We cannot remove hazardous materials such as asbestos, liquid paint, propane tanks, motor oil, chemicals, or biohazardous waste. For everything else — furniture, appliances, yard waste, construction debris, and more — we've got you covered. Text us a photo at (619) 841-4193 if you're unsure.
