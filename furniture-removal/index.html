FAQ

Furniture Removal FAQs

Common questions about our furniture and appliance removal service.

How much does furniture removal cost in San Diego?

Furniture removal in San Diego starts at $99 for a single item and ranges up to $400+ for multiple large pieces. Final pricing depends on the number, size, and weight of items. We provide a free upfront quote — you only pay when the job is done.

Do you remove mattresses in San Diego?

Yes! We remove mattresses of all sizes — twin, full, queen, king, and California king. We properly dispose of all mattresses in compliance with San Diego County regulations and recycle materials whenever possible.

Can you remove refrigerators and large appliances?

Absolutely. We remove all major appliances including refrigerators, freezers, washers, dryers, stoves, ovens, and dishwashers. We handle all the heavy lifting and ensure responsible disposal, including proper refrigerant handling where required.

Do you donate or recycle removed furniture?

Whenever possible, yes. We sort usable items and donate them to local charities, recycle metal and electronics, and only send items to the landfill as a last resort. Clean disposal is part of every job.

How quickly can you remove furniture in San Diego?

Same-day furniture removal is often available. Call or text us at (619) 841-4193 to check availability. We typically schedule 2-hour arrival windows and can frequently accommodate last-minute requests.
