FAQ

Yard Waste Removal FAQs

Common questions about our yard and green waste removal service.

What yard waste do you remove in San Diego?

We remove tree branches, stumps, landscaping debris, old fencing, dirt and soil, rocks, pavers, hot tubs, spas, lawn furniture, and all other outdoor debris. If it's in your yard, we can likely haul it away.

How much does yard waste removal cost in San Diego?

Yard waste removal starts around $99 for small loads and ranges up to $400+ for larger amounts. Final pricing depends on volume and type of materials. We provide free upfront quotes with no hidden fees.

Do you remove hot tubs in San Diego?

Yes! Hot tub and spa removal is one of our specialties. We carefully break down and haul away hot tubs of any size. The process typically involves draining, cutting into manageable sections, and hauling away all debris. Contact us at (619) 841-4193 for a free hot tub removal quote.

Can you remove tree stumps?

We can haul away cut or broken-up stumps. Full stump grinding (in-ground extraction) requires specialized equipment — contact us to discuss your specific situation and we'll help point you in the right direction.

What is green waste disposal and how do you handle it?

Green waste includes plant material such as grass, leaves, branches, and landscaping trimmings. We haul all green waste to approved disposal and composting facilities in San Diego County, keeping it out of the landfill whenever possible.
