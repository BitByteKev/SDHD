Maintenance Services FAQs

Common questions about our property maintenance service in San Diego.

What maintenance services do you offer in San Diego?

We offer a range of property maintenance services including general cleanups, hauling and debris removal, pressure washing, yard clearing, and general handyman-style tasks for homes and businesses throughout San Diego County.

Do you offer pressure washing in San Diego?

Yes. We offer professional pressure washing for driveways, patios, sidewalks, fences, building exteriors, and more. Pressure washing is available as a standalone service or as part of a larger property cleanup package.

Can you help with ongoing property cleanup in San Diego?

Yes. We work with residential and commercial property owners who need regular maintenance cleanups — monthly, quarterly, or on an as-needed basis. Contact us to discuss a recurring maintenance schedule that fits your needs.

How much do maintenance services cost in San Diego?

Maintenance service pricing varies based on the type and scope of work. Small cleanup jobs start around $150, while larger or recurring maintenance packages are custom-quoted. Contact us for a free assessment and upfront quote.

Are your maintenance services available for rental and commercial properties?

Yes. We regularly work with landlords, property managers, HOAs, and commercial property owners throughout San Diego County. We can handle tenant turnover cleanups, regular property maintenance, and more.
