FAQ

Estate Cleanout FAQs

Common questions about our estate and home cleanout service.

How much does an estate cleanout cost in San Diego?

Estate cleanout pricing depends on the size of the property and volume of items. Small garage or storage unit cleanouts typically start around $200–$400. Full-home or hoarder cleanouts can range from $800 to $2,000+. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote.

What is included in an estate cleanout?

Our estate cleanout service includes removing all unwanted furniture, appliances, clothing, personal items, and debris from the property. We sort items for donation, recycling, or disposal, and leave the space broom-swept clean.

Can you handle hoarder cleanouts in San Diego?

Yes. We have experience with all levels of hoarding situations and approach every cleanout with professionalism and respect. We can handle full-property hoarder cleanouts in San Diego, including multi-room and multi-story properties.

How long does an estate cleanout take?

A standard estate cleanout typically takes 1–2 days depending on property size and volume. Severe hoarding situations may require additional time. We provide realistic timelines during the free quote process.

Do you donate usable items from estate cleanouts?

Yes. During every cleanout we sort through items to identify anything that can be donated to local charities or recycled. Only items with no reuse value go to disposal. This is part of our standard service at no extra charge.
