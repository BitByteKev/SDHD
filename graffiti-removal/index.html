FAQ

Graffiti Removal FAQs

Common questions about our graffiti removal service in San Diego.

How much does graffiti removal cost in San Diego?

Graffiti removal pricing depends on the surface type, size of the area, and severity of the graffiti. Most residential jobs start around $150–$300, while larger commercial surfaces may cost more. Contact us for a free quote — same-day assessment available.

How quickly can you respond to graffiti in San Diego?

We offer same-day graffiti removal response throughout San Diego County. Quick removal is important to deter repeat vandalism — the sooner it's removed, the less likely it is to return. Call or text (619) 841-4193 for urgent requests.

What surfaces can you remove graffiti from?

We remove graffiti from brick, concrete, stucco, wood, metal, vinyl, painted surfaces, fences, storefronts, dumpsters, and more. Our team uses surface-appropriate techniques to restore the area without causing damage.

Does graffiti removal damage the surface underneath?

When done correctly, graffiti removal should not damage the underlying surface. We use professional-grade products and techniques tailored to each surface type. In some cases, affected areas may need a fresh coat of paint to fully restore the original appearance.

Do you handle commercial graffiti removal in San Diego?

Yes. We provide commercial graffiti removal for storefronts, office buildings, warehouses, schools, and municipal properties throughout San Diego County. We can also set up recurring removal agreements for properties that are frequent targets.
